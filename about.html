About Me

My name is Jagger Dane Verdugo and I am a designer/developer from Arizona who specializes in UI, UX, and accessibility.

I attended Arizona State University (ASU) fresh out of high school, and graduated in 4 years, receiving a Bachelor of Science in Informatics in the spring of 2024. Throughout those years, I also earned a certificate in "Computer Gaming" at ASU that provided a skill set in game development, including knowledge in game engine architecture, programming, game design, art design, and graphics.

I've always had great passion for design and art ever since I was young—like, really young. Back then, I gravitated towards sketching user interfaces with colored pencils and designing rules for video games that only existed in my head (I'm sure my mom still has some of those drawings somewhere). Now, I'm working towards channeling these early endeavors and sharing my creativity with the world.
