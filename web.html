The Record Room - Redesigned Prototype

April 2023 — Visit

For the culminating project in my Intro to Human-Computer Interaction at Arizona State University, I worked in a group of four to conduct a study testing the usability of a chosen website and redesigning it using principles, guidelines, and theories learned in class in order to improve it. A total of twelve participants were part of the study and were divided evenly into two groups: original website testers and redesigned website testers. The redesigned website was designed with an emphasis on creating better efficiency, functionality, and learnability, and the results proved that our redesign significantly improved the usability of the website.
